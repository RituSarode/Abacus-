Benefits of Learning Abacus

High Calculation Speed with Accuracy

Performing Mathematical calculations with speed & accuracy helps in performing well in exams. Time saved can be used in solving other problems.

Makes Mathematics an Interesting Subject

Teaching abacus is the best way to make maths an interesting subject because once kids start doing mental calculations, they fall in love with this subject.

Develops Power to Concentrate

Abacus exercises help students to improve their focus, which is critical for great academic performance across all subjects.

Develops Observation & Visualization Power

Abacus exercises improve observation skills, making students capable of analyzing situations quickly and responding efficiently.

Develops Memory Power

Abacus training enhances memory by combining concentration, observation, and imagination, allowing students to perform complex calculations effortlessly.

Develops Confidence and Self-Esteem

By excelling in speed and accuracy in calculations, students gain confidence and are motivated to excel in other fields.
