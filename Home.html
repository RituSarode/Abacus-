What is an Abacus?

Abacus is a simple tool or a hardware used for performing rapid arithmetic calculations. Calculation based on abacus was invented in the ancient times and is now widely used as a brain development program. It consists of a rectangular frame holding a number of vertically arranged rods, on which beads slide up and down.
